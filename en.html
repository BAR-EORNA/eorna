"Inspired by Mr. Kimura's vision of creating a bar in Shinjuku 3-chome that anyone could comfortably drop into, I took over EORNA with that same spirit. EORNA aims to be a hideaway bar where even solo guests feel at ease. Whether it's your first visit or your hundredth, please enjoy whisky and cocktails at your own pace. We look forward to welcoming you in Shinjuku 3-chome."
